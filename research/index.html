Research

My research is based primarily in fluid dynamics and in particular hydrodynamic stability theory. I am interested in laminar flow control techniques and have looked at oscillatory flows over rotating disks and flat plates.


CV | pdf


Projects

  • PhD Thesis (2014 - Present)
    • Supervisor: Dr. Chris Davies
    • To investigate the stabilising effects of adding modulation to a rotating disk boundary layer.

  • MMath Thesis (2010 - 2014)
    • Supervisor: Dr. Chris Davies
    • To investigate the connection between the local and global stability analyses of an oscillatory Stokes layer.

  • Undergraduate Project (CUROP) (2013)
    • Supervisor: Dr. Chris Davies
    • To develop an efficient numerical algorithm for solving a system of nonlinear time-dependent differential equations.
