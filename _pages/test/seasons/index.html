SEASONS | BRIDGEND COLLEGE

A modern restaurant run by students

About Us

Seasons is operated by a dedicated team of some of the best industry trained chefs in Wales, along with our learners who work together to prepare and serve a wide selection of meals including classical and modern cuisine.

We use the best quality local produce along with some herbs and vegetables supplied by our land-based learners at Pencoed Campus and serve Fairtrade Puro barista coffee.

ABOUT

The restaurant is open to everyone, so come along and sample the excellent cuisine and fantastic service. Seasons is staffed with the College's catering lecturers and students. The College students are studying to progress into the Tourism and Hospitality sector.

LOCATION

Situated within Bridgend College's Cowbridge Road Campus, Seasons is perfectly located for access from public transport.
